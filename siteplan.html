MyPadiMan Website Plan

Site Name

MyPadiMan

MyPadiMan is a dynamic marketplace connecting users with local errand runners, skilled artisans, and local businesses. The name reflects a friendly, neighborhood helper, and the concept was inspired by successful platforms such as Uber and Amazon.

Site Purpose

The purpose of MyPadiMan is to provide an interactive and responsive platform where users can request errands, hire artisans, shop from local businesses, and engage in real-time communication. The site will feature secure payments, a dynamic dashboard, and an integrated wallet system.

Scenarios

  • How can I quickly find a trusted errand runner in my neighborhood?
  • Where can I view the services and reviews of local artisans?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #022142 (Blue) – Used for buttons, navigation, and key accents.

Secondary Color: #F18F01 (Vibrant Orange) – Used for highlights, success messages, and call-to-action areas.

Background: A clean white or light neutral color to ensure readability and focus on content.

Typography

Heading Font: Poppins – to be used for titles and headings for a modern, clean look.

Body Font: Roboto – to be used for paragraphs and body text, ensuring high readability.
